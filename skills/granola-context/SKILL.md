# granola-context

Someone is mid-work and needs to check what was discussed or agreed. Maybe they're implementing something and want to confirm the approach. Maybe they're writing a doc and need to recall who said what. Maybe they just can't remember whether a decision was actually made or only floated.

Use `query_granola_meetings` with their question. Return a concise answer — the decision or information, which meeting it came from (title and date), and who was involved. If the answer came up across multiple meetings, note whether the thinking evolved or stayed consistent.

Keep it brief. The person asking is in the middle of something and wants a quick, citable answer — not a report.
